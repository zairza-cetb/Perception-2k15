About

Perception is the annual Techno-Literary-Management Fest of College of Engineering and Technology, Bhubaneswar! Perception was created with a virtuous intent of uniting brilliant minds across various institutions and it has met its expectations since the recent years. It has been a miscellany of contests, of ideas, creativity and numerous abilities while knitting these together with the threads of fun and enjoyment. Perception 2k15 comes up with the promise of being even better, with more challenges and more fun.It'll consist of a blend of various categories of events ranging from technical events as Robotics, Literary and Oratory events, Management Games and what not. Adding to that it will also have talks by exceptional speakers from across the country and exciting workshops as well. Some come and enjoy this exquisite festival of vivid competitions and guaranteed frolic.

Kaarobaar

Karobaar brings together bright and curious minds from diverse disciplines and tests raw management potential – can they innovate, compete and perform in teams? This peep into the world of management coats the rigor and vitality of a career in management with a strong dose of fun. The objective of this game is to give them a glimpse of MBA education and also to help them in making the right decision in future. Karobaar consists of following modules:- Marketing, Finance, Operations, HR. The Karobaar challenge is to simplify MBA jargon so that it is understandable by under-graduate students. We have games in Finance, Marketing, Operations, HR but everything is fine tuned to suit the participants who don't know about these much.More

Register

If you are reading this then you probably made one of the best decisions of your life to attend the fun-filled three day experience at C.E.T, Bhubaneswar. You're now going to be a part of the techno-management and literary fest of our college, PERCEPTION 2K15, and the C.E.T. family earnestly welcomes you. Now, we hope you enjoy your time here as well as learn as much as you can. In lieu, we ask for the following.

  1. You need to register with an amount of Rs.200(without accommodation facilities) or Rs.500(with accommodation facilities) to attend any event on any day from among 21st,22nd and 23rd February, 2015.
  2. This above registration will be done at the registration desk set up within the college premises. You are required to produce your college/school ID card in order to get your PERCEPTION registration ID. You are also advised to carry it along throughout the fest.
  3. There is also a help desk where all your queries, pertaining to any event, will be addressed. You will also be heard if you have to file a complaint. Necessary action is guaranteed.
  4. Though we want you to enjoy your time with us to the fullest, we also expect cooperation from all our participants. If indecency is observed, in any case, necessary action will be taken against the responsible participant and s/he won't be allowed in the college premises further.
We whole-heartedly wish you a happy-PERCEPTION 2K15 and more!
